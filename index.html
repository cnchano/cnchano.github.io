About Me:

Hello, I'm Charles Chano, a proud Mexican-American originally from the greater Los Angeles area, California, USA.

My career has equipped me with a diverse background spanning dynamics and control in aerospace and automotive applications to automation and quality in aerospace manufacturing. Leveraging my interdisciplinary knowledge between computer science, mechanics, and electronics.

But my love for robotics, controllers, and AI has always been in front of mind.

In 2024 I graduated with a Master's degree in Robotics Engineering from Warsaw University of Technology, Warsaw, Poland. Focusing on control and collaboration of decentralized UAV agents.

In my professional achievements, I've optimized workflows, enhanced product and system quality, and ensured maintainability. My decision-making process is rooted in data-driven insights and my team’s past experiences, enabling me to successfully implement impactful changes.

I take most pride in my work that benefits everyone involved.
